Frequently Asked Questions

Can I take individual weeks instead of the full program? +

Yes! Each week is designed to work standalone. You can purchase any 1-6 weeks based on your needs. However, we recommend taking weeks in order (1-6) for the best learning progression. Individual weeks are $350-400 each.

How much time does this really take per week? +

Exactly 8 hours per week, broken down as:

  • 3.5 hours: Pre-recorded modules (watch anytime)
  • 1.75 hours average: Live expert sessions (Tuesday/Thursday/Friday)
  • 1.5-2 hours: Hands-on labs and practice
  • 0.75-1.25 hours: Quizzes and project work
What if I can't make live sessions? +

All live sessions are recorded and available on-demand within 24 hours. You can watch them on your schedule. However, we strongly recommend attending live sessions to engage with peers and ask questions in real-time.

Do I need any prior AI experience? +

No. This program is designed for marketing professionals with 5+ years experience but no AI background. We start with fundamentals in Week 1 and build from there. All tools are free (or free trials) so you can learn as you go.
